### Based on the videos and the reading material, how would you define a data scientist and data science?

A **data scientist** is a professional who uses a combination of statistical, analytical, and computational skills to extract insights and knowledge from data. They are proficient in programming, machine learning, and data visualization, and they are able to tackle complex problems by analyzing large datasets. A data scientist's role often involves designing algorithms, building predictive models, and interpreting data to help organizations make informed, data-driven decisions.

**Data science** is the interdisciplinary field that combines techniques from computer science, statistics, and domain expertise to analyze and interpret complex data. It encompasses a wide range of tasks, including data cleaning, data exploration, statistical analysis, machine learning, and data visualization. The goal of data science is to turn raw data into actionable insights that can be used to solve problems, make predictions, or optimize processes in various industries.

In essence, **data science** is the overall process and methodology, while a **data scientist** is the expert who carries out that process.
